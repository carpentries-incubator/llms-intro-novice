This lesson explores some key terms and concepts in artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on "generative AI" technologies.

After completing this lesson, learners will be able to:

* Describe factors that influence the output produced by a large language model.
* Connect important concepts in artificial intelligence.
* Explain how bias can be introduced into a large language model.

:::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::: prereq

### Prerequisites
This lesson is aimed at people with no prior experience of programming/computational research.
It assumes some foundational knowledge of probability and computers.
